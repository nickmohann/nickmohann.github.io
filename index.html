Our Programs

Youth Development

Comprehensive training program designed for young athletes aged 6-12 to develop fundamental soccer skills.

  • Basic ball control and dribbling
  • Passing and receiving techniques
  • Team coordination and sportsmanship
  • Fun-focused learning environment

Competitive Training

Advanced program for serious players aged 13-18 looking to take their game to the next level.

  • Advanced tactical training
  • Position-specific skill development
  • Physical conditioning
  • Mental preparation and strategy

Summer Camps

Intensive week-long camps combining skill development with fun activities and team building.

  • Daily training sessions
  • Scrimmages and mini-tournaments
  • Guest coach workshops
  • Awards and recognition

Get to Know Us

Total Soccer Training, nestled in the heart of Frisco, Texas, is dedicated to shaping young athletes through comprehensive soccer programs.

We strive to provide a nurturing environment that fosters growth, teamwork, and a love for the beautiful game. Our coaches are committed to enhancing each player's skills and passion for soccer.

With years of experience and a proven track record of success, we're proud to be the premier soccer training destination in the area.
